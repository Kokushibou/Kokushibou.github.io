Education’s role in the Holocaust
Changes in education will affect an entire generation. Since Hitler takes complete
control of Germany, he has made a thorough reform of the education system for minors.
First of all, Hitler only allows male students to go to formal school because He wants his
students to join the army after graduation. Moreover, he turns the school into a military
training camp. In this way, he can carry out military management and education for young
people. (Sigward) At the same time, Hitler introduces a lot of racial discrimination in
schools. In his books, he portrays Jews as sinners in the world. He also isolates all students
of Jewish origin from other students. Hitler does not allow “Aryan” to come into contact
with “non-Aryan”. (Sigward) Therefore, Hitler’s reconstruction of the education system
makes education play the most role in promoting Holocaust by ensuring the total blind
loyalty of children, strengthening their hatred of Jews, and removing their ability to have
empathy.
First off, Hitler’s style of education stiffens Children’s minds to make them be total
loyalty. From the rules made by Dr. Bernhard Rust: decrees that in Nazi schools the norm is
physical edulearly shows that. (Sigward) In this course system, the key to judging whether
the students are excellent enough is their sport scores. If students spend a lot of time on
exercise, they don’t have enough time to study other subjects. Such a monotonous course
selection seriously affects the wide range of knowledge learned by students. Under the
influence of the concept of sports priority, students will have no interest and ability in
learning other content. At that time, students are like a group of robots. Although they are
strong physically, they are mentally empty and easy to be led by a certain person or an idea.
Hitler understands this very well. He publicizes Nazi superiority through various forms of
expression, such as radio, drama, film, art, church, especially schools. In March 1933, he
issues the first education law, stipulating that “German culture must be treated thoroughly”.
(Graham) Another evidence is geography had become geopolitics. In every moment of
school, you can see the words about the superiority of Nazis. (Graham) Such an exaggerated
way of education making these students with spiritual emptiness engrave with the thought of
“I’m proud to fight for the Nazis”. They are no longer interested in accepting or learning real
history and facts. In their eyes, Hitler is God and Nazi is their faith. Through these actions,
Hitler successes in ossifying the thoughts of this generation of students and making them his
tools. These students are so ruthless when they see the Jews being slaughtered because they
think they are doing correct things. They can’t judge right from wrong. They will do
whatever Hitler says.
In education, Hitler not only publicizes himself and the Nazi party, but also does a lot
for making German students more disgusted with Jews. Hitler adds “race science” to the
course of biology to tell students the concept of race. (London Jewish Cultural Centre) At
the same time, Hitler gives different names to people of different races. People of pure
German origin are called “Aryan”. In school, “Aryan” is not allowed to contact and
communicate with “non-Aryan”. (Sigward) In some states of the country, Hitler isolates
individuals of Jewish descent and makes them study in separate schools. (John) These
actions make German students fully aware that they are different from other races. Moreover,
Hitler also makes up fake information to vilify the image of Jews. For example, he believes
that Jews are the cause of Germany’s chaos and economic depression. (Graham) He also
says that the all most every Jew is Communist: “the Jews established a cruel dictatorship
over the Russian people and now try to extend it to the rest of Europe and the rest of the
world.” (Graham) Because the students at school have no resources or will to understand
real history, they easily believe Hitler’s words. The image of Jews in this generation is
collapsed. This hatred of false information continues to the Holocaust. In their minds, these
Jews are criminals, so it is right to seize and kill them.
Another important point that we should consider is making them have no sympathy.
We can see from the words from American educator Gregor Ziemer: A teacher is not spoken
of as a teacher (Lehrer) but an Erzieher which means they are more like commands.
(Sigward) As physical education is a key course, we can imagine how much physical torture
students suffer every day. According to the school time table at that time, about 80% of
